FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

How do I get a U.S. shipping address?

Create a Shop2GY account. Your customer ID and assigned U.S. address will appear in your dashboard.

Can I ship perfume?

Perfume is treated as a restricted or hazardous item and may require special handling, additional fees or a different shipping method.

Does the quote include customs duties?

No. The website estimate covers Shop2GY shipping and selected services. Customs duties and taxes are determined separately.

Can businesses receive volume pricing?

Yes. Business accounts can receive negotiated rates based on frequency, weight and service requirements.
